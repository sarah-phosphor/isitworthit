Turkey vs United States

Turkey 1  ·  United States 1
Group D
Does it matter? Yes.
What changes?
Whether the U.S. wins the group.
Why?
The U.S. controls the group with a win. Dropped points could change who they play next.
Expected result
The U.S. is favored.
If not
They still may advance, but the path gets less clean.
“May” — a draw only sends the U.S. through outright if Turkey doesn't win their next match. A loss puts them into the third-place math.
